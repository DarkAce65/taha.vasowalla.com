Augmented Reality Sandbox
Andover Innovation Lab

An augmented reality sandbox with real time topographic projections and water and lava simulations. Our SARndbox was designed by us, the students of the Andover Innovation Lab, constructed from wood and utilized an Xbox Kinect, a short throw projector commandeered from our principal's office, and a custom built computer.

Our modified code base is available on GitHub.
Instructions and Tutorial were provided by UC Davis.
