Eagle Project

Photos

Boloian Reservation
190 Chandler Road, Andover MA

My Eagle Project improved the appearance of a small parking lot at the head of the Boloian Trail on Chandler Road in Andover. With the help of other scouts, parents, and friends, I built a wood and cable fence to define the boundaries of the parking lot. In addition to the fence, my project included repainting the parking lines and constructing a wooden bench for the trailhead. The project totaled 164 man hours and a few months of planning to reach completion.

Special thanks to The Home Depot and Alfred J. Cavallaro Inc for their generous donations of supplies and materials.
